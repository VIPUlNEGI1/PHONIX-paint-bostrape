Legacy of Innovation in Construction

Phoenix has a specified product range of construction chemicals that are cost efficient, environment friendly and one that adds significant value to any infrastructure project. While Phoenix is headquartered in the enterprising city of Ahmedabad, Gujrat, India, the organization has nurtured relationship with clients across the country.
