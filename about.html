About Us

Precision-Crafted. Performance-Proven.

At Radi-Lok, we don’t just manufacture components — we engineer confidence. With a deep legacy of serving oil & gas, petrochemical, defense, and transportation sectors across 15+ countries, we are known for delivering zero-defect products, batch after batch. Founded with a vision to bring global quality out of India, Radi-Lok is a vertically integrated manufacturer specializing in stainless steel instrumentation tube fittings, valves, pipe fittings, hoses, flanges, and pressure components — all built in-house.

“When I started Radi-Lok, I wanted to prove that an Indian manufacturer could meet — and exceed — global standards without excuses. We didn’t chase certifications to impress, we earned them to perform. Every fitting we make carries our name, and our name means something.” — Kalpesh Kumar, Founder

Our Founder

Engineering Excellence

Radi-Lok was founded by Kalpesh Kumar, a visionary who believed Indian engineering could stand shoulder-to-shoulder with the world’s best. His mission wasn’t just to manufacture — it was to innovate. His focus on quality, traceability, and manufacturing depth has shaped Radi-Lok into a world-class operation trusted by EPCs and OEMs globally. From humble beginnings hand-loading machines, we’ve grown to operate 5+ CNCs, lathes, and advanced testing facilities, yet our hands-on mindset remains unchanged.

What Sets Us Apart

  • 100% In-House Production – From forging to final packaging, every process is under our roof.
  • Swagelok-Level Interchangeability – Designed to drop into global systems without modification.
  • Proprietary Silver Plating – Smooth torque, anti-galling, and tested to last.
  • World-Class QC Lab – Hydrostatic, pneumatic, PMI, burst, roughness, torque, VMM & more.
  • Traceability, Always – MTC 3.1, RMTC, Heat Charts, and QAPs — one click away.
