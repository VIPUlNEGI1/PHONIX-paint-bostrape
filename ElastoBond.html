Product Overview

ElastoBond - High-Performance Acrylic Polymer

ElastoBond is a specially formulated acrylic polymer designed to enhance the properties of cementitious compositions. Mortar prepared with ElastoBond is exceptionally durable and flexible, offering superior elongation properties. It boasts excellent adhesion to a wide range of substrates, including wood, concrete, brick, and metal, making it an ideal choice for various construction and repair applications.

Features

Application & Advantages

  • Bonding Agent: Provides strong and reliable bonding for various materials.
  • Cementitious Waterproofing: Ideal for creating impermeable barriers.
  • Good Workability: Achieves excellent workability even at reduced water-cement ratios.
  • Superior Bonding and Adhesion: Ensures strong and lasting adhesion to diverse substrates.
  • High Tensile Strength: Offers robust structural integrity.
  • Excellent Water Impermeability: Creates a highly effective barrier against water penetration.
  • Two-Component Tile Adhesives: Suitable for enhancing the performance of two-component tile adhesives.

Application

Waterproofing Applications

ElastoBond exhibits excellent elongation properties when mixed with cement and cement mortar, making it highly suitable for large-scale waterproofing projects. It can be effectively used for:

  • Terrace waterproofing
  • Basement waterproofing
  • Podium slab waterproofing
  • Sunken areas
  • Swimming pool areas

Its unique formulation ensures a durable and flexible waterproof barrier for critical areas.

Storage & Packaging

Storage

Store between temperatures of 5°C and 40°C. Keep containers tightly closed when not in use to maintain product integrity.

Shelf-Life

18 Months from the date of manufacturing, provided it is stored under shade, away from direct heat and sunlight, and well protected from freezing.

Pack Size

Available in HDPE Drums ranging from 20kg to 200kg, offering flexibility for various project sizes.
