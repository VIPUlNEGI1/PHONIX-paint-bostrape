Phoenix Chemicals delivers advanced solutions for enhancing structural integrity and waterproofing. Our PrimeBond+, alongside products like HydroSeal and PU TopCoat, provides exceptional adhesion and durability, making it ideal for a wide range of construction applications.

PrimeBond+: Acrylic Emulsion for Cementitious Modification

PrimeBond+ is a specially formulated acrylic emulsion designed to modify cementitious compositions, including cement primers, crack filling, anchor and grout, waterproofing, and damp proofing. Mortar prepared with PrimeBond+ is durable, tough, and ideal for thin-section applications. It offers excellent adhesion to substrates such as wood, concrete, brick, and metal, ensuring robust performance in demanding environments.

At Phoenix Chemicals, we prioritize delivering high-performance, eco-friendly solutions. Our PrimeBond+ product is designed for seamless integration with cementitious mixtures, ensuring enhanced durability and water resistance.
