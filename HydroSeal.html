Product Overview

HydroSeal - Reactive Hydrophobic Solution

HydroSeal is a water-based organic polymer designed as a reactive, penetrative, water-soluble hydrophobic solution. Its low molecular size allows deep penetration, offering excellent water repellency and enhancing surface durability. Ideal for a wide range of surfaces, HydroSeal improves water resistance and protects substrates from weathering, making it a versatile solution for construction and maintenance applications.

Contact Us
Features

Application & Advantages

  • Reactive & Penetrative: Penetrates 5 to 7 mm into brick and plaster surfaces due to low molecular size.
  • Long-Lasting Protection: Shields substrates from deterioration caused by weathering.
  • Hydrophobic Agent: Acts as a penetrating solution for concrete, cement plaster, gypsum, and other surfaces.
  • Excellent Film Formation: Enhances surface strength through robust film formation.
  • Alkali & Water Resistance: Provides strong resistance while allowing the surface to breathe.

Application

Waterproofing Applications

HydroSeal is suitable for improving water resistance on a variety of surfaces, including concrete, cement plaster, gypsum, brick, and more. Its reactive and penetrative nature ensures deep protection, making it ideal for both interior and exterior applications.

  • Coverage: Ideal consumption ranges from 120 grams to 200 grams per square meter, depending on the surface type.
  • Application: Apply to clean, dry surfaces free from dust, oil, or other contaminants. Ensure proper surface preparation for optimal penetration and performance.
