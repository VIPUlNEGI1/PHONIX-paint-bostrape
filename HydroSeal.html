HydroSeal

  1. Home
  2. HydroSeal Waterproofing

Phoenix Chemicals, based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, is a trusted leader in the construction industry, offering innovative solutions for waterproofing and roof protection. Our HydroSeal product is a cutting-edge, water-based organic polymer designed to enhance water resistance across various surfaces, ensuring long-lasting protection and durability.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification, Phoenix Chemicals ensures that HydroSeal, alongside products like ElastoWhitePU and PU TopCoat, meets stringent quality and environmental standards. Our solutions are engineered for versatility, providing robust protection for residential and commercial projects across India.
