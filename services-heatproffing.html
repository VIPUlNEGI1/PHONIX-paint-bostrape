Our Brochures

    Phoenix Chemicals, based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, offers cutting-edge heatproofing membranes to protect roofs and structures from extreme heat. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified products ensure energy efficiency, durability, and environmental sustainability.

Our hybrid PU-based chemical, ElastoWhitePU, and hybrid PU-bituminous product, ElastoBlack, provide superior heat reflection and insulation, reducing cooling costs. We tailor our heatproofing solutions to meet specific project needs, ensuring long-lasting protection. Contact us at 7906 830 840 or visit B-21, Sarkhej Circle, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, for advanced heatproofing solutions.
