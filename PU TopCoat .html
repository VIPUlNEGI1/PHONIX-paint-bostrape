PU TopCoat

  1. Home
  2. PU TopCoat Waterproofing

Phoenix Chemicals, headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, is a leader in the construction industry, delivering innovative roof fixing solutions. Our products, including PU TopCoat, utilize advanced construction chemicals to provide durable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective waterproofing solutions. Specializing in high-performance membranes, we ensure roofs are protected from harsh weather, enhancing longevity and performance.

Certified with ISO 9001:2015, our Quality Management System guarantees that PU TopCoat, alongside products like ElastoWhitePU and HydroSeal, meets the highest standards of quality and sustainability. Our solutions are tailored for diverse applications, ensuring robust protection against leaks, cracks, and environmental stress for residential and commercial projects across India.
