Product Overview

AddiCrete - Integral Liquid Waterproofing Compound

Phoenix AddiCrete is a specially formulated integral liquid waterproofing compound composed of surface active plasticizing agents, polymers, and additives. It is designed as an additive for cement concrete, mortar, and plasters, enhancing cohesion and preventing segregation. AddiCrete meets the requirements of IS:2645-2003, ensuring high-quality performance for waterproofing applications.

Contact Us
Applications

Areas of Application & Features

Areas of Application

Waterproofing of concrete and sand-cement mortars used in:

  • Basements
  • Roof slabs and screeds
  • Water tanks and water retaining structures
  • External plastering
  • Bathrooms and balconies
  • Sumps and drains

Features & Benefits

  • Corrosion Resistant: Enhances concrete cohesion, protecting steel from corrosion.
  • Compatibility: Easily dispersible and compatible with concrete/mortar mixes.
  • Permeability: Reduces water permeability in concrete.
  • Strength: Maintains setting time and compressive strength within IS:2645-2003 limits.
  • Shrinkage: Reduces shrinkage crack development in plaster and concrete.
  • Workability: Improves workability of freshly mixed cement concrete.
  • Durability: Increases durability by enhancing concrete waterproofing.

Application

Method of Application & Precautions

Method of Application

  • Charge cement and aggregates to the concrete mixer as per the mix design, mix in dry state for 1–2 minutes.
  • Start adding 75–80% of the mixing water and mix for 2–3 minutes.
  • Add Phoenix AddiCrete as per the recommended dosage into the remaining mixing/gauging water, then add to the concrete mixer and mix for another 2 minutes.
  • Place the concrete or apply plaster as needed.
  • Cure the applied mortar or concrete as per good construction practices.

Precautions & Limitations

  • Do not add Phoenix AddiCrete directly to dry cement and aggregate mix.
  • Maintain water-cement ratio as low as possible.
  • Do not increase the dosage of Phoenix AddiCrete beyond the recommended amount.
Details

Dosage, Packing, Storage & Safety

Dosage

100ml–200ml per 50 kg bag of cement.

Packing

Available in 20-liter containers.

Shelf Life & Storage

Best before 2 years from the date of manufacture when in sealed pack and stored under proper conditions. Store in a cool, dry place.

Health & Safety Precautions

Use rubber hand gloves and safety goggles while using Phoenix AddiCrete. In case of contact with skin, wash with plenty of water. Keep out of reach of children.
